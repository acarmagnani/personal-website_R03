This project was developed in 2023 while working at the renowned architecture firm Isay Weinfeld, and it was designed by a team composed of myself, Eduardo Lisboa, Felipe Zene Motta , and Lucas Jimeno Dualde. In this case, in addition to assisting in the conception of the work, I was responsible for its modeling. The project consists of a composition of wavy translucent glass pieces that make up an entrance door.
The textured translucent glass served as the inspiration for this work by combining the sensation of privacy and mystery with permeability and spaciousness.
